# Constitution: Playbook

## Mission
Make team appointment management and attendance tracking effortless for sports clubs — especially those participating in official Jugend und Sport (J+S) programs that require Anwesenheitskontrolle.

Inspired by Spielerplus.de, but with a modern cross-platform approach.

## Business Goals
- Allow coaches/managers to plan events and appointments
- Enable attendance control (Anwesenheitskontrolle) for J+S compliance
- Provide players with a simple way to confirm/decline attendance
- Reduce admin overhead for volunteer team managers

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Sports team coaches and managers (B2C)
- **Secondary:** Players / team members (confirming attendance)
- **Context:** Amateur and youth sports clubs in Switzerland (J+S context)

## Core Requirements
- Team management (create teams, invite members, assign roles)
- Appointment / event scheduling
- Attendance tracking per event (confirm / decline / no-response)
- Attendance reports for J+S compliance
- Push notifications for upcoming events and reminders
- Mobile-first (iOS + Android), full web access for all users

## Constraints
- Must work offline or with poor connectivity (sports venues)
- J+S compliance requirements may dictate specific data fields
- GDPR / Swiss data protection law applies (user data, minors)
